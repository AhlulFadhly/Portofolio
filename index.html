Beyond Application

In 2024, as part of my Bangkit Academy team, I developed an innovative Indonesian Sign Language translator app called Beyond. This app is designed to foster better communication between the deaf and hearing communities. Beyond allows you to effortlessly translate sign language with both speed and accuracy. Its standout feature is the ability to convert photos or images into text. Simply snap a picture of a hand making a sign or point your camera at someone using sign language, and Beyond will provide the translation for you.
